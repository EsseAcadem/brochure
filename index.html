Process & How to book
  • Free courses: Delivered by the Essentia Academy or Trust. Ask your manager, then e-mail the contact on the specific course card.
  • Paid courses: These require manager support and approval from the committee. We announce new application rounds from time to time—please keep an eye on the DT&I Newsletter for news.
  • Apprenticeships: These are levy-funded and have no closing date, so you can enrol at any time. You will need your manager's approval and a commitment to spend 6 hours a week on your studies. If you are interested, please get in touch with the Academy team.
