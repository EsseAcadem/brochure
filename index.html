Process

How booking works

  • Free courses are delivered by the Essentia Academy or part of the Trust offer. Ask your manager, then email the contact on each card.
  • Paid courses require a training request form, manager support, and committee approval (GSTT windows: end of June & end of October; review in January).
  • Apprenticeships are levy-funded; require role fit and manager approval; Trust form starts enrolment.
