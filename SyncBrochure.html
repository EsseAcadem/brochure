Process & How to book
  • Free courses: Delivered by the Essentia Academy or Trust. Ask your manager, then e-mail the contact on the specific course card.
  • Paid courses: Browse a training area, select the exact provider course that suits your need, then submit the DT&I training application. Courses require manager support and approval, so please do not book directly with the provider.
  • Apprenticeships: These are longer, work-based development programmes and are separate from the paid technical training application rounds. They do not follow the same application deadlines, so you can take time to explore the options and discuss them with your manager. A short introduction is available before you browse.

Apprenticeship information

Thinking about an apprenticeship?

A quick overview of how apprenticeships differ from shorter technical training courses.

Separate process No training-round deadline

You can express interest throughout the year, so there is no need to rush.

Longer-term learning Usually 12–48 months

Learning takes place alongside your role, with reviews and a final assessment.

Workplace support Manager agreement is needed

The programme should fit your role and include agreed learning time at work.

Your path to an apprenticeship

Emailing the Academy starts an enquiry. The usual journey then follows these stages.

1. Explore and discuss

Choose an apprenticeship that fits your role and career plans, then have an initial conversation with your manager.

Select a number to see each stage. Timings vary depending on checks, provider availability and start dates.

You are welcome to browse the options and take time to consider what may suit you. When you are ready, the Academy will guide you and your manager through the process.

Apprenticeships follow their own route. They are not part of the technical training application round. When you are ready, explore the options and contact the Academy rather than submitting a technical training application.
